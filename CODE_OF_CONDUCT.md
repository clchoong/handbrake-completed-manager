# Community code of conduct

HandBrake Completed Manager welcomes respectful participation from people of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, patient, and specific.
- Respect differing experience, viewpoints, and accessibility needs.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and ideas—not people.
- Protect privacy: do not post personal media, private paths, credentials, or unsanitized databases and logs.
- Accept maintainer decisions about project scope and file-safety requirements.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publication of private information, and sexualized or otherwise inappropriate conduct are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when conduct is harmful to the community or users.

For sensitive conduct or security reports, use GitHub's private **Report a vulnerability** form in the repository Security tab. Good-faith reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as a volunteer-maintained project allows.
